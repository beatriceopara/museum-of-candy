MUSEUM OF CANDY

Life is sweeter at the Candy Museum. The Museum of Candy will make its debut in London summer 2020. Welcome to The Museum of Candy, where you can explore the history of jawbreakers, make your own candyfloss and taste taffy twistedness. If you ever dreamed of nibbling your way through Candyland or scoring a Golden Ticket, you’ve come to the right place. Treat yourself to The Musuem of Candy, and let your tastebuds and your imagination soar!
